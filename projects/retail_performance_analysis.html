Business Intelligence Project

Retail Performance Analysis

An end-to-end business intelligence project analysing retail performance across products, customers and regions using Microsoft Excel, MySQL and Microsoft Power BI. The project combines SQL analysis, interactive dashboards and evidence-based recommendations to support data-driven decision-making.

Project Overview

Business Background

The retail company sells a wide range of products across multiple categories and geographic regions. Management required greater visibility into business performance to identify opportunities to improve profitability and operational efficiency.

Business Problem

Management had limited visibility into the factors influencing profitability. The company needed to understand which products, customer segments and regions contributed most to profit and where underperformance existed.

Project Objective

Analyse retail sales data to evaluate profitability across products, customers and regions, identify key performance drivers and develop data-driven recommendations to improve business performance.

Dataset

The Superstore Sales Analysis dataset contains three related tables: Orders, People and Returns. It includes transactional retail data covering the period from 2023 to 2026.

Business Questions

  • How did sales and profit change over time?
  • Which product categories, sub-categories and products generated the highest profits?
  • Which regions generated the highest profits, and which regions underperformed?
  • Which customer segments and individual customersgenerated the highest profits?
  • Which regional manager oversaw the highest-profit region?
  • Which regions had the highest return rates, and how did returns affect profitability?

Methodology

Step 1

Data Exploration

Reviewed the dataset in Microsoft Excel to assess its structure, formatting, missing values, duplicate records and data type consistency.

Step 2

SQL Analysis

Imported the dataset into MySQL and developed SQL queries using joins, aggregation, grouping, filtering and distinct-order calculations.

Step 3

Power BI Development

Established table relationships, created DAX measures and built three interactive Power BI dashboards using KPIs, slicers and business visualisations.

Step 4

Business Interpretation

Interpreted the findings, identified key performance drivers and developed practical, evidence-based business recommendations.

Key Business Insights

Sustained Business Growth

Total sales increased from $494,040.21 in 2023 to $745,567.53 in 2026, while total profit increased from $51,684.30 to $95,926.35.

Technology Led Product Performance

Technology generated the highest total sales of $839,893.28 and the highest total profit of $146,543.38.

Loss-Making Sub-Categories

Tables, Bookcases and Supplies recorded overall losses despite generating sales, indicating possible pricing, discount, supplier-cost or demand issues.

Consumer Was the Most Valuable Segment

The Consumer segment contributed 46.66% of total profit, making it the organisation's most valuable customer segment.

West Was the Strongest Region

The West region generated the highest total profit of $110,798.82, although it also recorded the highest return rate of 11.56%.

Business Recommendations

Expand Investment in Technology Products

Increase targeted marketing, inventory planning and product portfolio investment in high-performing Technology products.

Review Loss-Making Sub-Categories

Investigate pricing, discount policies, supplier costs and customer demand for Tables, Bookcases and Supplies.

Investigate Returns in the West Region

Analyse product quality, fulfilment processes and customer expectations to reduce returns while maintaining strong regional sales.

Replicate Successful Regional Strategies

Evaluate the West region's sales, operational and customer engagement practices for implementation in lower-performing regions.

Strengthen Consumer Customer Retention

Use loyalty programmes, targeted marketing and personalised promotions to retain high-value Consumer customers.
